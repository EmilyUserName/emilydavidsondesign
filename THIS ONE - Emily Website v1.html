About me

Builder at heart,
developer by choice.

I don't have an elevator pitch. I have calluses, a paintbrush, a lesson plan, and a keyboard — and I will deploy all of them in the same week without blinking.

I come to web development from a genuinely hands-on background — I've run my own acupuncture practice, done construction work, and most recently built data integrations in hospitality tech. That means I ship practical things, not just pretty prototypes.

Based on the Maine coast, I build full-stack web products independently, from architecture through deployment. My neighbors call me when the answer is complicated. My clients do too.
